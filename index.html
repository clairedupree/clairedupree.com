about

Claire Dupree is an artist-turned-software developer. Originally a classically-trained painter, graduated in 2016 with a Bachelors of Fine Arts from Salisbury University, MD, USA. As an artist, she posseses a honed eye for design and user experience, informed also by her background in hospitality. Always a lover of science and engineering, Claire followed her hobbies into a career in tech.

Claire currently works as a Front-End Developer in Maryland. She applies her skills in communication and customer service to client interfacing, perfomring product demonstrations, and fostering a collaborative workplace.
